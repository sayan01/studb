IITMPOD Student Database

Lookup all the IITMPOD Students by their email id, group id, or house name.

Search

Examples

The search bar uses regex to match the queries, advanced search queries can thus be performed easily.

Group ID

...

gives only 3 digit groups

..

gives 2 or 3 digit groups

^..$

gives only 2 digit groups

20.

gives 3 digit groups starting with 20

20.*

gives 2 or 3 digit groups starting with 20

^29$

gives only group 29

Email ID

21F1

gives first batch students

6567|3422

gives two results

0{5}

gives emails with 5 or more zeros

House

Sundarbans

gives Sundarbans members

Sunderbans

gives nothing
